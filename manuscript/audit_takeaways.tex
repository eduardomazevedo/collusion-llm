Out of hundreds of thousands of transcripts, the LLM was able to pull out a select few containing collusive content. 
This content takes a variety of forms including expressing a need for reduced industry capacity, noting that it has 
taken the lead in cutting supply and saying competitors need to do their part, and putting forth a call for the 
industry to show pricing discipline. At the same, there is a high rate of false positives. The LLM often did not 
catch the nuanced difference between a firm describing its own supply or capacity reduction, showing pricing 
discipline, or some other form of constrained conduct and the firm calling on the industry to do so.
